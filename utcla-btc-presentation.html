How It Works

From agreement to treasury — five steps

  1. 1
    Resource Agreement Signed — Mining company and UTCLA agree on terms. Payment clause specifies Bitcoin only.
  2. 2
    UTCLA Issues a Bitcoin Address — A unique wallet address is generated. Only the Council can spend from it.
  3. 3
    Mining Company Sends Bitcoin — Direct on-chain transfer. No bank. No wire transfer. No intermediary.
  4. 4
    Funds Held in Vultisig Treasury — Bitcoin arrives and is secured in the multi-signature Council wallet.
  5. 5
    Council Authorises Distributions — By collective signature, funds flow to Nations, communities, and approved projects.
