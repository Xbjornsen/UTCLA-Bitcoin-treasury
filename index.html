How It Works

From agreement to treasury — five steps
  1. 1
    Resource Agreement Signed — payment clause specifies Bitcoin only
  2. 2
    UTCLA Issues a Bitcoin Address — only the Council can spend from it
  3. 3
    Mining Company Sends Bitcoin — direct on-chain, no bank, no wire transfer
  4. 4
    Funds Held in Vultisig Treasury — secured in the multi-signature Council wallet
  5. 5
    Council Authorises Distributions — by collective signature, funds flow to Nations and projects
